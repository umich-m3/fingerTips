Optic Nerve And Optic Chiasm Disorders

Optic Pit Neuropathy

    • What is it?
    • Congenital hole in optic disc
    • Attributed to faulty embryogenesis
    • Non-progressive visual field defect caused by absent optic nerve axons

    What does it look like?
    • Focal excavation in optic disc infero-temporally
    • Corresponding steep-margined visual field defect
    • Serous detachment of macula sometimes

    What else looks like it?
    • Early glaucoma
    • Optic disc coloboma

    What should you do?
    • Look for optic pit as explanation for macular fluid or unexplained visual field defect

    What will happen?
    • Optic pit and visual field defect remain stable
    • Macular serous detachment may require photocoagulation or vitrectomy
    • Trap: optic pit is often misdiagnosed as glaucoma and unnecessarily treated
