The Eyes Have It

Welcome to a unique teaching tool on vision and eye care created by Jonathan D. Trobe, M.D. Videos and photos reinforce core knowledge and procedures

Instructional Mode

In this mode, you will review topics that cover a range of eye care problems.

Quiz Mode

In this mode, you will confront case problems that assess your knowledge of eye care.
